### üß¨ STRUCTURAL PATTERNS (YAML)
ideology:
  - Total rejection of logic, war, and bourgeois values
  - Destruction of "Art" with a capital A
  - Embrace of chance, chaos, and absurdity
  
techniques:
  - Sound Poetry ("Gadji beri bimba")
  - Cut-Up / Collage (Newspaper clippings)
  - Simultaneous Poems (Multiple voices reading different things at once)
  - Typography as visual art
  - Readymades (Context shifting)

vocabulary:
  - Nonsense syllables (ba, um, zii)
  - Industrial/Mechanical terms mixed with organic
  - Shocking obscenities or banalities
